# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) as its Code of Conduct.

All contributors, maintainers, and participants in this project are expected to uphold these standards. Please read the full text at the link above.

## Summary

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone. We expect all participants to:

- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Be respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Show empathy towards other community members

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project team at **conduct@cebus.ai**.

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. The project team is obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter of an incident.
